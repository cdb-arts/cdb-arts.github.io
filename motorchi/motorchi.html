Motor Chi

This is a personal project that I am currently working on. I am a motorcycle blogger, I take photos and videos of my travels. I also edit videos and images for other motorcyclists. I recently created some videos of my friend doing a road trip. I use my Instagram account (chiluvbears) to update my experience as well as random projects. I created a website, HTML email templates, a decal, a couple of digital posters, and a logo.
