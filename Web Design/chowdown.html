Live Ordering

Choosing your meal is easier than ever before! Users can view their restaurant's menu, add to their order, and quickly confirm with the server right from their phone!

Flavor Filter

Finding a restaurant shouldn't be difficult, so we added our super smart flavor filter! Users will be able to find restaurants based on food, location, pricing, and reviews from the people they know. You can even securely sync allergy information and to help find the best fit for you!

Easy Pay

The average person spends 29 hours a year just waiting for the check. It's time to end that trend. Thanks to ChowDown's Easy Pay, you'll be able to split the tab, save your receipts, and even tip right from your phone! Use a regular credit card, Apple Pay, or simply press the 'Cash' option to call your server and save time.
