    Motor Vent

    Motor Vent was a class project to exercise that taught how to make UX and UI designs. I decided to do something that not only fits my interest but can be a long-term project. Motor Vent is a social network that Motorcyclist can use to find events, share pictures and message with one another, and find sales from big or local companies.

    My resources were people are a part of groups like Bike Life Massachusetts and New England Riders on Facebook. I also used random people in coffee shops. I had a lot of fun with this project and was great to use Experience Design to make the prototype. I hope to have this open during the winter time.
